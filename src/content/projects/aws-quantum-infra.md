3 years managing non-scientific infrastructure at the AWS Center for Quantum Computing. My day-to-day involves everything from coordinating with construction teams on lab build-outs to managing IT infrastructure and inventory systems. It's less glamorous than the physics but absolutely essential; someone has to make sure the dilution fridges have the right plumbing and the cleanroom stays clean.
